Signing in (Super user level)

  • Signing into the software will consist of email address and password established at implementation for super user (company representative).

Creating administrators

  • The super user (company representative) is the only user that can create administrators.
  • Both super user / administrators will assign testers assessments.

Changing your password on the first sign-in

  • Upon signing in for the first time, you will be prompted to change your password.
  • Type your new password in the allocated fields.

Forgot your password

  • If you have forgotten your password, you can reset it by selecting "Forgot Password."
  • Instructions will arrive via email to reset your password.

Setting Change (Super user / company representative)

  • Pass and fail scoring defined by the type of exams.
  • Skills Checklist are measured by selection 0–4 level of experience by tester.
  • Bundle testing that includes Annual exams will default a passing score: 80%
  • All Annual exams will default a JC and OSHA/HIPPA standard passing score: 80%
  • All other exams will default a standard passing score: 75%
  • Notification settings will be selected at implementation.

Reports

  • The score report tab will allow you to view, share, save (to your desktop), and print.
  • Testers access by clicking on the view button next to the completed test.
  • The super user / administrator(s) have access to all reports across organization via the report menu.

Change a tester password

  • Go to tester file.
  • Press the three dotted bar.
  • Select change password and share the new temporary password with tester.

Disable a tester

  • Go to tester section.
  • Select the tester.
  • Go to three dotted bar and select disable.
  • To view all disabled testers, go to the disable icon found in the tester section.
  • To enable, follow the same process under the disabled tab.

Cancel Subscription

  • Send Support team cancellation notice within 30 days.
  • Cancellations are processed within 72 hours.

Contact EscoreIt Support

  • Functionality & Testing error.
  • System nonresponsive.
  • Send ticket by email to technicalsupport@escoreit.com
  • Request will be managed within 24 hours.

Definition of the annual safety testing required by OSHA, HIPAA and Joint Commission

Annual Test (All clinicians)

Includes: For all clinical healthcare professionals

  • Advanced Directives
  • Body Mechanics / Ergonomics
  • Emergency Management Preparedness
  • Environmental Safety
  • Fire Safety
  • Hazardous Chemicals
  • HIPAA
  • Infection Control / Blood Borne Pathogens
  • Abuse
  • Advanced Care Planning
  • Domestic Violence
  • Pain Management
  • Patient Restraints
  • Sexual Harassment
  • Workplace Violence

Annual Safety Test (Allied)

Includes: For all Allied Professionals

  • Age Specific
  • Cultural Diversity
  • Ethics for Healthcare
  • National Patient Safety Goals (Allied)
  • Patient Rights

Annual Safety Test (Non-Nursing)

Includes: Medical Assistant & Certified Nursing Assistant Professionals

  • Age Specific
  • Cultural Diversity
  • Ethics for Healthcare
  • 2011 National Patient Safety Goals (Non-Licensed)
  • Patient Rights

Annual Test (Nursing)

Includes: All Nursing and LVN/LPN healthcare professionals

  • Age Specific
  • Cultural Diversity
  • Ethics for Healthcare
  • Key National Patient Safety Goals (Nursing)
  • Patient Rights
  • Preventing Medication Errors

Other

Required by OSHA National Safety Patient Guide (NSPG)

  • NSPG Allied & Non-licensed
  • NSPG Nursing

Also provided separately are the tests for the convenience of sending individually.
